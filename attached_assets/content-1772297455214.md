## Overview

A minimalist stills campaign for a niche perfume line, captured in soft natural light.

A visual ode to minimalism and memory, _Scent & Silence_ captures the quiet elegance of Laventura’s latest perfume line. Shot entirely in soft natural light, the series explores the interplay between fragrance and form — delicate bottles resting in shadow, textured surfaces bathed in morning glow. Each frame evokes a feeling rather than a product: stillness, intimacy, and the unspoken language of scent.
